Why Gelephu?

Gelephu is more than a city—it is a paradigm shift. Designed from the ground up around the values of Gross National Happiness, it is Bhutan’s response to a future that demands balance between growth and well-being.
“In Gelephu, prosperity is measured not by skyscrapers, but by inner peace, equity, and harmony with nature.”
With its strategic location on the Indo-Bhutan border and an international airport underway, Gelephu positions investors at the center of an emerging market corridor. Over 5.5 million annual visitors are expected by 2065 (Statista, 2023)—offering scalable revenue streams across tourism, hospitality, logistics, and real estate.
Bhutan’s political stability, transparent governance, and climate resilience (World Bank, 2020) make it an unparalleled location for long-term, ethical, and profitable investment.
