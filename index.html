Investing in Gelephu: The Vision

Gelephu is not just a city—it is a new development model. Rooted in Bhutan’s Gross National Happiness framework, it champions balance between progress and wellbeing.
“In Gelephu, prosperity is measured not by skyscrapers, but by inner peace, equity, and harmony with nature.”
Located on the Indo-Bhutan border, Gelephu is positioned to become a major economic corridor. With a new international airport and planned rail links to Northeast India, it connects South and Southeast Asia. By 2065, it is projected to attract over 5.5 million annual visitors (Statista, 2023).
Bhutan’s political stability, transparent governance, and robust climate policy (UNDP, 2023) strengthen its profile as a future-ready investment hub.
